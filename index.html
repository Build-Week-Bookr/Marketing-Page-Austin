Bookr is Social

What makes us special is our community. We created Bookr as a social-first medium where our users could connect with each other. This means that you get to connect with like-minded readers and share your joy of books. Our community also powers our book algorithm by voting up or down good books. We strive to make our community friendly, inclusive and fun! Enjoy!

Read our users' favorite reviews, or submit yours

Our large catalogue of book reviews means we're the finest site on the internet for book reviews. Almost every book you can think of, we have a review for that! That means you can just sit back and relax and read any of our reviews, or add an anonymous/named review of your own!
